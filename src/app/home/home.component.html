The Very Bad Game

Here are the rules of The Very Bad Game, Mkay?

First Rule: You don't talk about the game.

Second Rule: You don't talk about the game.

Third Rule: Once you receive a card, you will judge how BAD is that situation, sequencing that in your card deck. If you misjudge, well tough luck.

Fourth Rule: You can check the numbers on the right to help you identify where you should fit your card. The higher the number, the worse the situation.

Fifth Rule: Every team starts with 3 cards.

Sixth Rule: You need collect 7 cards to win the game.

Seventh Rule: If the game runs out of cards, the team(s) with the most cards in their possession win(s).

Eighth and Final Rule: You already play it in your daily life, and now you have to play it here. Life is tough: get over it.
