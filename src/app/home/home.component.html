The Very Bad Game

Here are the rules of The Very Bad Game, Mkay?

First Rule: You don't talk about the game.

Second Rule: You don't talk about the game.

Third Rule: Every team starts with 3 cards, in ascending order, which have a situation and an index of “misery”: The higher the number, more miserable is the situation.

Fourth Rule: On each turn, you will receive a card and you will judge how BAD that situation is, putting it your hand while maintaining the sorting order.

Fifth Rule: If you put the card in the right order, you keep it. Otherwise, you lose it.

Sixth Rule: You need to collect 7 cards to win the game.

Seventh Rule: If the game runs out of cards, the team(s) with the most cards in their possession win(s).

Eighth and Final Rule: You already play it in your daily life, and now you have to play it here. Life is tough: get over it.
