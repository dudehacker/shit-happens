Sh!t Happens

Here are the rules of Sh!t Happens, Mkay?

First Rule of SH: You don't talk about SH.

Second Rule of SH: You don't talk about SH.

Third Rule of SH: Once you receive a card, you will judge how BAD is that situation, sequencing that in your card deck. If you misjudge, well tough luck.

Fourth Rule of SH: You can check the numbers in the left to help you identify where you should fit your card. The higher the number, the worst is the situation.

Fifth Rule of SH: Every team starts with 3 cards.

Sixth Rule of SH: The maximum number of cards per team is 7

Seventh Rule of SH: If the game runs out of cards, the number of cards of each team will be counted, and the winner will be the one (or ones) with more care cards in their possession.

Ninth and Final Rule of SH: You already plays it in your daily life, and now you have to play it here. Life is tough: get over it.
