Sh!t Happens

Here are the rules of Sh!t Happens, Mkay?

First Rule of SH: You don't talk about SH.

Second Rule of SH: You don't talk about SH.

Third Rule of SH: Once you receive a card, you will judge how BAD is that situation, sequencing that in your card deck. If you misjudge, well tough luck.

Fourth Rule of SH: You can check the numbers on the right to help you identify where you should fit your card. The higher the number, the worse the situation.

Fifth Rule of SH: Every team starts with 3 cards.

Sixth Rule of SH: You need collect 7 cards to win the game.

Seventh Rule of SH: If the game runs out of cards, the team(s) with the most cards in their possession win(s).

Eighth and Final Rule of SH: You already play it in your daily life, and now you have to play it here. Life is tough: get over it.
